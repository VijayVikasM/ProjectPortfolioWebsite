Hello my name is Vijay Vikas Mangena

I'm a Robotics Graduate

As a graduate of the MSc. Robotic Systems Engineering program at RWTH Aachen University, I’ve had the opportunity to dive into diverse fields like Robotics, AI, Machine Vision, and Computer Vision. I find fulfillment in exploring a space where machines don’t just process data—they expand our understanding of what’s possible through innovative approaches. At Bosch Automotive Electronics, I gained hands-on experience in machine vision, optics, and industrial digitalization, which gave me invaluable insights into real-world applications. One highlight was my role in developing a synthetic data generation pipeline, an exciting project that Bosch deemed impactful enough to pursue a patent application. While I’m proud of these achievements, I see myself as an ongoing learner, always refining my skills and experimenting with several technical projects to push my own limits. I’m driven by a curiosity for complex problem-solving and aspire to build even more ambitious projects in the future—ones that challenge current standards and help shape the next wave of intelligent technology...
